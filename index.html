Defcee

Defcee (Adam Levin) is a rapper, published poet, and teaching artist originally from River Forest, Illinois. He recorded and released his first album, One Eye Open, at the age of 17 and has since performed in major cities across the U.S. and internationally in Panama, Mexico, and the United Kingdom.

Defcee released his last full-length project, Out From Under, in December 2011; he is currently working on his second solo album, Damn Near Grown, featuring guest appearances and production from NoName Gypsy, Saba, and Otis of electric soul duo Milo & Otis. It is set for release in 2014.
